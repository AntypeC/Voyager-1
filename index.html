Voyager 1

    Quick Info

  1. Launched date: September 5, 1977
  2. Distance from Earth: 23,823,119,199 kilometers
  3. Mass: 722 kilograms (1,590 pounds)
  4. Power source: Three RTGs
  5. Propulsion: One main engine and four small attitude control thrusters
  6. Data transmission rate: 160 bits per second
